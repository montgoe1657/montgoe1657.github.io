ADG Asset Management QuickBooks Sales Tax Integration Terms of Use

Effective date: June 12, 2026

This application is an internal tool used by ADG Asset Management LLC and authorized personnel to retrieve accounting information from QuickBooks Online for sales tax reporting, reconciliation, and related accounting review.

Authorized Use

Use of this application is limited to authorized ADG Asset Management personnel and advisors. The application may only be used for legitimate accounting, tax compliance, and internal reporting purposes.

QuickBooks Data

The application may access QuickBooks Online company information, invoices, payments, accounts, reports, tax codes, and related accounting records needed to prepare and review sales tax workpapers.

No Public Service

This application is not offered as a public software product or service. It is maintained for internal business use only.

No Warranty

The application is provided for internal workflow assistance. Users remain responsible for reviewing generated workpapers, confirming tax rates, verifying remittances, and ensuring filings are complete and accurate before submission.

Contact

Questions about this application may be directed to ADG Asset Management LLC.
