ADG Asset Management QuickBooks Sales Tax Integration Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the ADG Asset Management QuickBooks Sales Tax Integration handles information accessed through QuickBooks Online.

Information Accessed

The application may access QuickBooks Online accounting data needed for sales tax reporting and reconciliation, including company information, invoices, payments, accounts, general ledger report data, sales tax liability report data, tax codes, and tax rates.

Purpose of Use

QuickBooks data is used only to prepare internal Florida sales tax workpapers, identify paid customer invoices, review sales tax collected, and compare sales tax remittances against accounting records.

Storage

OAuth tokens and generated workpapers are stored locally in the authorized user's ADG Asset Management workspace. Generated files may include accounting data exported from QuickBooks Online for internal review.

Sharing

The application does not sell QuickBooks data and does not share QuickBooks data with unrelated third parties. Data may be reviewed internally by authorized ADG personnel and professional advisors for accounting and tax compliance purposes.

Security

Access to the application and generated files should be limited to authorized users. OAuth credentials, client secrets, refresh tokens, and generated accounting workpapers should not be publicly shared.

Data Retention

Generated files may be retained as accounting and tax support records according to ADG Asset Management's internal recordkeeping needs. OAuth access can be revoked through Intuit or QuickBooks Online account settings.

Contact

Questions about this policy may be directed to ADG Asset Management LLC.
